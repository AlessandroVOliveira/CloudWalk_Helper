## Stratus Blockchain

Developed by CloudWalk, Stratus is a high-performance, secure, scalable, and open-source blockchain designed for global payment networks.

### Key Features

#### High Scalability
- Processing up to 1,800 transactions per second (TPS)
- 160 million transactions per day
- Potential for infinite growth through sharding and multi-raft consensus models

#### Ethereum Compatibility
- Integrated with Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM)
- Allows creation of smart contracts
- Advanced functionalities for financial transactions

#### Enhanced Security
- Permissive blockchain where only authorized nodes can participate
- Controlled and safe environment with trustworthy institutions as validators
- Validator node structure ensures security

#### Architecture
- Layered architecture with JSON-RPC for communication
- RocksDB for optimized storage
- Sharding and multi-raft consensus model for exponential capacity expansion

### Use Cases
- **Financial Institutions**: Streamline transactions and reduce costs
- **Developers**: Build and deploy smart contracts effortlessly
- **Enterprises**: Customize financial operations and enhance security

### Open Source
Stratus's open-source nature fosters innovation and allows global collaboration, making it an ideal solution for businesses seeking secure, scalable, and customizable blockchain infrastructure.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is CloudWalk?
CloudWalk is a fintech company creating the interplanetary payment network, democratizing the financial industry through AI, blockchain, and innovative payment solutions.

### What is InfinitePay?
InfinitePay is CloudWalk's flagship payment platform in Brazil, offering POS machines, smartphone payment (InfiniteTap), payment links, and digital accounts with rates up to 50% lower than competitors.

### Is InfinitePay available for individuals (CPF)?
Yes, InfinitePay offers InfiniteTap for individuals who don't have a CNPJ, allowing them to make sales using their CPF and smartphone.

### What is JIM?
JIM is InfinitePay's AI assistant that helps with financial management, creates marketing campaigns, makes payments, reminds about commitments, and provides business insights 24/7.

### What is Stratus?
Stratus is CloudWalk's open-source blockchain designed for global payment networks, capable of processing 1,800 TPS with Ethereum compatibility.

### What payment methods does InfinitePay accept?
InfinitePay accepts debit cards, credit cards (installments up to 12x), and Pix (with zero fees).

### How quickly do I receive my money with InfinitePay?
Depending on your plan, you can receive money instantly or in 1 business day.
